    Products Explanation

    Facial Cleansers

    Facial cleansers are skincare products designed to remove dirt, oil, makeup, and other impurities from the face.
    They come in different forms such as gels, creams, foams, or liquids, and are typically applied with the hands or a washcloth.

    Haircare products

    Haircare products such as shampoos and conditioners are used to clean and maintain healthy hair.

    Conditioner, on the other hand, is a product that is used to moisturize and detangle the hair. It typically contains ingredients such as proteins, oils, and silicone that help to smooth the hair cuticle and provide a protective layer to prevent damage.

    Fragrances

    Fragrances, also known as perfumes or scents, are aromatic compounds that are used to give a pleasant and distinctive odor to various products such as perfumes, colognes, soaps, lotions, and household cleaning products.
    Fragrances are typically composed of a mixture of natural and synthetic aromatic compounds, and they can be categorized based on their notes, which refer to the different scent families that they belong.
